Assess your Digital Readiness with our DEX Framework.

Tavant's Digital Experience (DEX) Framework provides a digital analysis and benchmarking of your marketing acquisition, and execution capabilities. The assessment analyzes publicly available data and questionnaire-based surveys to assess the strengths and gaps in your organization's go-to-market, conversion strategies and results. The framework covers an objective analysis of more than 200+ performance parameters to arrive at a DEX Quotient for lenders.
